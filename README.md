**AI For Financial Literacy**

**TrackWise App:**
TrackWise is an AI-powered financial literacy platform designed to help students and young entrepreneurs in ASEAN improve their financial awareness, budgeting habits, and financial resilience. The application focuses on transforming complex financial data into simple, personalized insights that help users make better financial decisions.

**Problem:**
Despite the rapid growth of digital financial services such as digital wallets and Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL), many individuals lack the financial literacy needed to manage these tools responsibly. This often leads to overspending, debt accumulation, and poor financial planning.

**Solution:**
TrackWise provides an AI-assisted platform that helps users understand their financial behavior through budgeting tools, personalized recommendations, and educational financial content.

**Key Features:**
Smart Budget Tracker – Monitor and categorize expenses to understand spending patterns.

AI Financial Coach – Provides personalized financial advice and spending insights.

Learning Hub – Educational modules on financial literacy topics such as BNPL risks, savings, business management and debt management.

Student & Entrepreneur Modes – Customized financial guidance depending on the user's role.

**Target User:**
High School/University Students
Young Entrepreneurs
